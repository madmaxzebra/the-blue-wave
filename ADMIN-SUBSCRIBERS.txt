HOW TO SEND ONE EMAIL TO ALL SUBSCRIBERS
----------------------------------------
1. Open the email-broadcast link above
2. Write Subject + Message
3. Click "Preview count only" to check how many will receive it
4. Type SEND in the confirm box
5. Click "Send to all subscribers"

Each person gets their own email (private — no visible recipient list).
Your test emails are excluded automatically.


LIMITS
------
Gmail SMTP: about 500 emails per day. If you grow past that, send in batches on different days.
