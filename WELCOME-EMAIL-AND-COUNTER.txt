WELCOME EMAIL + SUBSCRIBER COUNTER — quick fix guide
====================================================

PROBLEM
-------
Some visitors do not get a welcome email.
The subscriber counter does not always update for everyone.

WHY
---
1. Render (the-blue-wave.onrender.com) has NO mail configured yet.
   Check: https://the-blue-wave.onrender.com/api/mail-status
   Should show mailConfigured: true

2. Welcome emails rely on EmailJS from the browser.
   Some phones block this (privacy settings, ad blockers, strict networks).

WHAT WE FIXED IN CODE (upload new zip to Bluehost)
--------------------------------------------------
- EmailJS is tried FIRST (with one retry) — not Render first
- Global subscriber counter uses countapi.mileshilliard.com
  (same number for all visitors worldwide)
- Counter refreshes every 15 seconds

UPLOAD
------
Double-click: bluewave\UPLOAD-THEBLUEWAVEFANS.bat
Then upload thebluewavefans-upload.zip to Bluehost (extract in public_html)

BEST FIX FOR WELCOME EMAIL (Render dashboard — 5 minutes)
-----------------------------------------------------------
1. Go to https://dashboard.render.com
2. Open service: the-blue-wave
3. Environment → Add Variable:

   SMTP_HOST = smtp.gmail.com
   SMTP_USER = your Gmail address
   SMTP_PASS = Gmail App Password (NOT your normal password)
   ADMIN_EMAIL = email where you want signup alerts
   SITE_URL = https://www.thebluewavefans.com

   Gmail App Password: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

4. Save → Render redeploys (~5 min)

After this, /api/welcome sends proper welcome emails even when EmailJS fails.
