UPDATE EMAILJS WELCOME TEMPLATE (one-time, 5 minutes)
=====================================================

WHY
---
The old welcome email text lives in your EmailJS template online.
Our code now sends the NEW text, but EmailJS will ignore it until
you update the template to use {{html_message}}.

STEPS
-----
1. Log in at https://www.emailjs.com
2. Go to Email Templates
3. Open template: template_ax3qxyy
4. Replace ALL body content with this single line:

   {{{html_message}}}

   (three curly braces = insert HTML from our code)

   Or open emailjs-welcome-template.html in this folder and paste that in.

5. Set Subject to:

   {{subject}}

6. Save the template

7. Upload thebluewavefans-upload.zip to Bluehost (if not done yet)

8. Push backend to Render (for Gmail welcome emails from server)

TEST
----
Sign up with a new email. You should see the updated welcome with:
- "we have registered you with: your@email.com"
