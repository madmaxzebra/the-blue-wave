UPDATE EMAILJS WELCOME TEMPLATE (one-time, 5 minutes)
=====================================================

WHY
---
The old welcome email text lives in your EmailJS template online.
Our code now sends the NEW text, but EmailJS will ignore it until
you update the template to use {{html_message}}.

STEPS
-----
1. Log in at https://www.emailjs.com
2. Go to Email Templates
3. Open template: template_ax3qxyy
4. At the TOP of the template screen, find the field labeled "Subject".
   Click in it, delete the old subject text, and type exactly:

   {{subject}}

   (two curly braces each side — our code fills in the real subject line)

5. In the BODY area you will see {{{html_message}}} — that is NOT enough by itself.
   You must also clear the hidden editor:

   a) Click the "Edit Content" button (pencil icon, right side of Content area)
   b) In the popup toolbar, click the "</>" icon (View HTML / source code)
   c) Select ALL the HTML (Ctrl+A) and delete it
   d) Type only this one line:

      {{{html_message}}}

   e) Click OK / Save in that popup

   If you skip step 5, EmailJS keeps sending the OLD designed email
   (pink logo box, "officially on the list", "The Blue Wave Team").

6. Open the "Auto-Reply" tab — make sure it is empty / turned off.
   (Old text there can override your welcome.)

7. Save the template (top right)

8. Upload thebluewavefans-upload.zip to Bluehost (after we rebuild — new JS bundle)

----
